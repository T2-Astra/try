How to Maximize GitHub Contributions

Posted on May 15, 2023

GitHub tracks your contributions through various activities such as commits, pull requests, and issues. Here are some tips to maximize your contribution graph:

  • Make regular commits to your repositories
  • Create and review pull requests
  • Open and comment on issues
  • Contribute to open source projects

Understanding the GitHub Contribution Graph

Posted on May 10, 2023

The GitHub contribution graph shows your activity over the past year. Each square represents a day, and the color intensity indicates the number of contributions made on that day.
